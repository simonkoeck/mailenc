how to test documentation
  1. 01
    Have a PGP keypair in your mail client. Thunderbird (built-in OpenPGP), Mailvelope, GPG Suite, K-9 Mail, Proton Mail Bridge, or plain gpg + msmtp all work.
  2. 02
    Encrypt to my key. Most clients fetch it via WKD automatically. You can also download it.
  3. 03
    Make your own key discoverable via at least one of:
    • WKD on your domain (advanced or direct method)
    • The Autocrypt: header your client probably sends
    • Upload to keys.openpgp.org
  4. 04
    Send the encrypted email to the address above. We preserve the +token so the result streams back into this browser tab.
