How to test

  1. Have a PGP keypair in your mail client. Thunderbird (built-in OpenPGP), Mailvelope, GPG Suite, K-9 Mail, Proton Mail Bridge, or plain gpg + msmtp all work.
  2. Encrypt to my key. Most clients fetch it via WKD automatically. You can also download my public key directly.
  3. Make your own key discoverable via at least one of:
    • WKD at your domain (advanced or direct method)
    • The Autocrypt: header your client probably sends
    • Upload to keys.openpgp.org
  4. Send a PGP-encrypted email to the address above. We preserve the +token, so just reply-to or paste it in.
